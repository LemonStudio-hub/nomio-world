# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 15, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Nomio.World (hereinafter referred to as "the Service"). We deeply understand the importance of your personal information and will do our best to ensure the security and reliability of your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to all services you access through the Nomio.World platform.

## 2. Information Collection

### 2.1 Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we may collect the following information:

| Information Type | Description | Purpose |
|-----------------|-------------|---------|
| Username | Your chosen identifier during registration | Account identification, domain allocation |
| Password Hash | Encrypted password | Account security authentication |
| Origin URL | Your configured website address | Domain forwarding service |
| Email Content | Emails sent to your mailbox | Email hosting service |
| Access Logs | Service usage records | Service operation and security |

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We **do not** collect the following information:

- Real names
- ID numbers
- Phone numbers
- Personal addresses
- Payment information
- Cookies and trackers

## 3. Information Usage

We use the collected information only for the following purposes:

1. **Service Provision**: Providing you with domain and email services
2. **Account Security**: Protecting your account from unauthorized access
3. **Service Improvement**: Optimizing and improving our services
4. **Legal Compliance**: Complying with applicable laws and regulations

## 4. Information Storage

### 4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored in Cloudflare's global edge network, including:

- **D1 Database**: User information, email metadata
- **Workers**: Service runtime

### 4.2 Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **Email Data**: Stored in your mailbox, which you can delete at any time
- **Log Data**: Automatically deleted after 30 days

## 5. Information Sharing

We **do not** sell, exchange, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

- With your explicit consent
- Required by law or regulation
- To protect our legitimate rights and interests

## 6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

- **Transport Encryption**: All data transfers use HTTPS encryption
- **Password Protection**: Passwords are encrypted using PBKDF2 algorithm
- **Access Control**: Strict access permission management
- **Security Audits**: Regular security audits

## 7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access**: View the personal information we hold about you
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate personal information
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Data Portability**: Obtain your data in a common format

To exercise these rights, please contact us through GitHub Issues.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be published on this page with the update date indicated. We recommend that you review this policy regularly.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:
