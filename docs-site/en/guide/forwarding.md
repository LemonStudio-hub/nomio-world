# Email Forwarding

This chapter introduces email forwarding configuration and usage.

## What is Email Forwarding?

Email forwarding can automatically forward received emails to your specified external email.

## Configure Forwarding

### 1. Go to Email Settings

After logging in, go to mailbox page and click "Settings" tab.

### 2. Fill in Forwarding Email

Fill in your external email address in the "Forward Email" field.

### 3. Save Settings

Click "Save Settings" button.

## Forwarding Email

### Supported Emails

- Gmail
- Outlook
- Yahoo
- QQ Mail
- 163 Mail
- Any other email

### Examples

| Forwarding Email | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| your@gmail.com | Gmail |
| your@outlook.com | Outlook |
| your@qq.com | QQ Mail |

## Enable/Disable Forwarding

### Enable Forwarding

1. Go to email settings
2. Check "Enable Email"
3. Fill in forwarding email
4. Save settings

### Disable Forwarding

1. Go to email settings
2. Uncheck "Enable Email"
3. Save settings

## Forwarding Notes

### Forwarding Timing

- Forward immediately after receiving email
- Forwarding and receiving are synchronous

### Forwarding Content

- Forward complete email content
- Including attachments
- Preserve original sender information

### Forwarding Limits

- Each email is forwarded only once
- Failed forwarding is not retried
- Forwarding doesn't affect original email storage

## Forwarding Status

### View Forwarding Status

On email settings page, you can view:
- Whether forwarding is enabled
- Forwarding email address
- Storage usage

### Forwarding Failure

Possible reasons for forwarding failure:
- Forwarding email address is incorrect
- Forwarding email rejects
- Network issues

## Common Questions

### Is there a delay in forwarding?

Usually no delay, forward immediately after receiving email.

### Will forwarding lose emails?

No. Forwarding is performed on the basis of receiving emails, original emails are still saved in Namio.World.

### Can forward to multiple emails?

Currently only supports forwarding to one email. To forward to multiple emails, use external email's forwarding feature.

### Can the forwarding email be modified?

Yes, you can modify the forwarding email address at any time.

### After stopping forwarding, are previous emails still there?

Yes, stopping forwarding only affects newly received emails, previously received emails are still saved in Namio.World.
