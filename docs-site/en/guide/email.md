# Using Email

This chapter introduces how to register and use Nomio.World email.

## Register Email

### 1. Go to Mailbox Page

After logging in, click "Mailbox" in the left navigation bar.

### 2. Register Email

1. Click "Register Email"
2. (Optional) Fill in forwarding email
3. Click "Register Email" button

### 3. Registration Success

After successful registration, you will get `your-name@nomio.world` email address.

## Email Features

### Receive Emails

Any emails sent to `your-name@nomio.world` will be received and stored.

### Email Forwarding

You can configure a forwarding email, and received emails will be automatically forwarded to the specified email.

### Storage Space

Each user has 100MB email storage quota.

## Receive Emails

### Email Address

Your email address is: `your-name@nomio.world`

### Receiving Limits

- Maximum 5MB per email
- Maximum 3 emails per sender in 5 minutes
- Account frozen after 90 days of inactivity

### View Emails

1. Go to mailbox page
2. Click "Inbox" tab
3. Click email to view details

## Email Client

Nomio.World provides professional email client features:

### Mail List

- Pagination display
- Search function (search sender, subject, body)
- Filter function (all, unread, read, starred)
- Sort function (by time, size, sender, subject)

### Mail Detail

- Plain text and HTML display
- HTML email sanitization (XSS prevention)
- Star marking
- Read/unread status

### Batch Operations

- Batch delete
- Batch mark as read
- Batch mark as unread

## Email Forwarding

### Configure Forwarding

1. Go to mailbox page
2. Click "Settings" tab
3. Fill in forwarding email
4. Click "Save Settings"

### Forwarding Notes

- After enabling, received emails will be automatically forwarded to the specified email
- Forwarding email can be any email (like Gmail, Outlook)
- Leave empty to disable forwarding

### Enable/Disable

You can enable or disable email functionality at any time:
- Enable: Receive emails normally
- Disable: Stop receiving emails

## Email Settings

### Setting Options

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Enable Email | Enable/disable email functionality |
| Forward Email | Email forwarding address |

### Storage Usage

You can view storage usage on the settings page:
- Used space
- Total quota (100MB)
- Usage percentage

## Common Questions

### Can the email send emails?

The current version only supports receiving emails, not sending. You can set up "Send as" in clients like Gmail.

### Is there a storage limit for emails?

Each user has 100MB email storage quota. After exceeding, the oldest emails are automatically deleted.

### How to forward emails to other emails?

Fill in the forwarding email address in email settings, and received emails will be automatically forwarded to that email.

### Can the email domain be customized?

Currently only `@nomio.world` email is supported. Custom domain email support is planned for future versions.

### How long does it take for the email to be effective?

The email is immediately effective after registration. You can receive emails immediately.
